Who are we?

❮ ❯


People's Library is an American public library with headquarters in Washington.We re both an offline store and has one of the largest online library in the world. We have our offline stores in New York, Texas, Ontario in the US. We have our reach in other countries like UK, Spain, India, Belgium,China, Russia, Algeria, Austria, Japan. People's Library provides an option to purchase books as well as rent them by adding them into your online cart and probably reading them later. We ship to other countries either via Amazon or eBay or use Bluedart so your package gets to you, safe.


Our History

1998-

The idea was developed in 1998 by Mr David who wanted to provide education to people in form of libraries. This idea became a complete success when Mr David started collecting books and stalling it out resembling a library.

2003-

In January, 2003 Mr David recieved a funding of $2 Million by the Society of Education, US to legally build a public library.

2005-

In March, 2005 the first offline store of our many library chains was set up. It was named as People's Library.

2007-

After the massive success of the US store, stores were panned to be set in UK, Spain , Japan, Austria etc

2011-

The first web page of People's Library was set up online. This was on demand of public.

2017-

People's Library received its second round of funding of $133 million and stores were up in all the nations where it is presently.
